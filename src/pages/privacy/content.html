Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

ProofX is a small research site and code project. This policy describes the limited information handled when you visit the site or contact the maintainer.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information: email address and message content when you contact ProofX.
  • Basic usage data: non-sensitive site access information such as pages visited or referring pages, when collected by hosting or monitoring tools.

ProofX does not ask for financial information, biometrics, or sensitive personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

  • Respond to research or collaboration inquiries.
  • Maintain and debug the static website.
  • Understand whether public pages are loading correctly.

ProofX does not sell personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

The project may rely on third-party services such as GitHub, hosting providers, email providers, or basic monitoring tools. Those services process data under their own policies.

4. Retention

Contact messages are kept only as long as needed to respond or maintain context. You can request deletion by email.

5. Security

Reasonable care is taken with contact information, but no website or email workflow can guarantee perfect security.

6. Your Rights

To request access, correction, or deletion of contact information, email alkindi.proofx@gmail.com.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated as the site changes. The latest version will remain available on this page.
